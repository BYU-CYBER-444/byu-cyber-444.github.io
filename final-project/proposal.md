# Final Project Proposal
{: .no_toc }

**Due:** End of Week 8 &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Weight:** 5% of final grade
{: .fs-5 }

---

## Requirements

Submit a **1-page proposal document** (PDF or Word) to the LMS by the end of Week 8. The proposal must define:

1. **Infrastructure Scope** — list all servers (name, IP, OS, role)
2. **Target Compliance Frameworks** — specify which CIS Benchmark level and STIGs for each server
3. **Tools to Be Used** — list automation tools (Ansible, Terraform, etc.)
4. **Division of Responsibilities** — if working in a team of 2, who owns which server/deliverable
5. **Week-by-Week Milestones** — your personal timeline from Week 8 to Week 15

---

## Grading

The proposal is graded on completeness (all 5 sections present and specific). A brief instructor
feedback session will occur in Week 9 lab to confirm scope.

---

## Template

A proposal template is available on the LMS (`Capstone_Proposal_Template.docx`).
