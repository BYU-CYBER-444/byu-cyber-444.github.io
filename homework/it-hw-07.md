## Description

Complete the **IT Track Midterm Practice Exam** on Learning Suite before coming to class in Week 8. The practice exam is designed to help you identify gaps in your preparation before the actual midterm.

Topics covered mirror the IT Track Midterm:

- ITIL 4 framework: service management practices, change types, incident vs. problem management
- Data center fundamentals: Uptime Institute tiers, power architecture, cooling, physical security
- Linux network services: DNS (BIND9), DHCP, NFS, NTP configuration concepts
- Windows Server infrastructure roles: WSUS, DFS, backup strategies
- High availability concepts: active/passive vs. active/active, HAProxy, keepalived, RTO/RPO
- DNS security and email authentication: DNSSEC, SPF, DKIM, DMARC

You may retake the practice exam as many times as you like before the deadline. Learning Suite records your most recent attempt.
