## Description

Complete the online Syllabus Quiz on the LMS. The quiz covers key course policies including: grading breakdown, late work policy, lab attendance requirements, academic integrity policy, Final Project milestones, and required tools/software. Students must score **100%** — unlimited retakes are allowed until the deadline.

---

## Deliverable(s)

Completed LMS quiz submission. The system records your highest score automatically — no separate upload required.

---

## Grading Rubric


- Pass/Fail
- Score ≥ 80%: full credit (100 pts)
- Score < 80%: 0 pts until retaken and passed
