## Description

Complete the online Midterm Practice Exam on Learning Suite before coming to class on Oct 20. The practice exam contains example questions drawn from Weeks 1–7 and is designed to help you assess your readiness and identify any areas that need additional review before the midterm.

Topics covered mirror the actual exam:

- Linux and Windows Server hardening (CIS Benchmarks, DISA STIGs)
- Patch management pipelines and vulnerability scanning
- Ansible playbook structure and configuration management concepts
- Logging, auditd, and SIEM fundamentals
- Identity and access management (PAM, SSH, LDAP/AD)

You may retake the practice exam as many times as you like before the deadline. Learning Suite records your most recent attempt.
