# Lab Assignments
{: .no_toc }

50% of course grade · Lab reports are due **one week** after the lab session · See the [Schedule]({% link schedule/index.md %}) for kick-off weeks.
{: .fs-5 .fw-300 }

{: .important }
All labs must be conducted in your designated lab environment (local VMs or provided Proxmox instance). Scanning or testing systems you do not own is prohibited.
